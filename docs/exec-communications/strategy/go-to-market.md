# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Company size**: 200–5,000 employees
- **Industry**: SaaS, e-commerce, media/streaming, fintech
- **Tech profile**: 10M+ DAU, significant CDN spend ($50K+/year), engineering-led procurement
- **Pain signal**: CDN budget review, high origin compute costs, p95 latency complaints

## Sales Motion
1. **PLG seed**: Free tier (up to 3 edge nodes) for self-serve adoption and proof-of-value
2. **Land**: Single-team deployment via CPM Runtime license ($500/node/month)
3. **Expand**: Multi-region rollout, Model Hub Pro, Prediction API overage
4. **Enterprise**: Custom contract, SLA, dedicated support ($150K+ ACV)

## Channels
- Direct enterprise sales (primary, post-Series A)
- CDN partner marketplace (Cloudflare Workers marketplace, Fastly partner program)
- Developer community and OSS evangelism (secondary)

## Pricing
| Tier | Price | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| Starter | Free (3 nodes) | Proof-of-value |
| Growth | $500/node/month | Mid-market |
| Enterprise | $150K+ ACV | Fortune 500 |
