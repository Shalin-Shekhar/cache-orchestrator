## Revenue Assumptions

### CPM Runtime License ($500/node/month)
- Average nodes per customer: 12 (growth → enterprise)
- Assumed blended ACV at scale: $72K/year per customer
- Expansion: +3 nodes/quarter once production deployed

### Prediction API Usage ($0.18/1M calls)
- Included tier: 500M calls/month per customer
- Average overage at scale: 20% of customers, 2x overage
- Blended API uplift: ~$800/customer/month at maturity

### Model Hub Pro ($2K/month add-on)
- Attach rate: 35% of Growth+ customers by Year 2
- Retention: 92% annual

### Enterprise Contracts ($150K+ ACV)
- Assumes 15% of customer base becomes Enterprise by Year 3
- Average Enterprise ACV: $210K (custom deployment + SLA)
